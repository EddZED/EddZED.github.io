LifeCassette

LifeCassette automatically detects the sounds of laughter & excitement so you can easily capture your fun & authentic moments.

Listen to funny and spontaneous moments shared by people around the world, or capture your amazing memories by simply living in the moment! With LifeCassette, the sound of your laughs and emotions are automatically detected, so you can capture and share your special everyday moments without any effort!

Automatically detect laughs & emotions

We make it easy for you to capture the moments of your day in audio. You just press record and go on enjoying your time! LifeCassette extracts audio segments containing speech and uses AI to automatically detect the sounds of laughter, awww and excitement! Your conversations are then labeled with these emotions so that you can easily find your awesome moments and share them.

Share your fun moments or experience others

You’ve captured your fun moments and now it’s time to express yourself to the world! Post an audio snippet from a spontaneous conversation, or get more philosophical and share an intriguing discussion that made you realize something new! Share your happiness and share your wisdom! :)

You can share a full conversation or easily extract an audio snippet. You can even add a picture and give it more life!

Enjoy listening to amazing recordings shared by people from all over the world, giving you a glimpse of their funny moments and meaningful memories! With LifeCassette, you can experience a new kind of social media!

Privacy-focused design

LifeCassette is designed with privacy as its primary focus. Our AI and machine learning algorithms are optimized to run efficiently on your mobile device. All your recordings are processed locally so they can remain private and local to your phone. We also provide a secure cloud storage option, if you want to save memory on your device. Simply navigate to privacy and security in your app to choose the storage option. We have worked hard so you can be in full control!

Identify speakers

You can teach LifeCassette to recognize you and your friends with a little bit of teamwork! Your app finds new speakers in conversations and presents them to you. Once you label a few samples for each person, LifeCassette learns and starts detecting you, your friends and family in conversations, and automatically labels them for you! Now you can easily find the discussions you are looking for.

Keep a daily journal of conversations

You can also use LifeCassette to keep a journal of your daily interactions and conversations. Whether you want to remember a conversation you had with a friend or relive a fun memory, we make that easy for you. The library page keeps your recordings and labels them with the detected emotions and speakers, so you can easily find the conversation you are looking for.
