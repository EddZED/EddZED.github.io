Shabba –
It’s trivia for
charity

Race your way to glory and money in SHABBA, the new way to play trivia. In this fast-paced game, you answer five questions as quickly as you can to win. There are no eliminations, no hosts, and no waiting. Just fun.

Think you’re quick enough? All you have to do is finish in the top 20% of participants to win and DOUBLE your money. If you don’t, then you help donate with 50% of game profits going to a new charity each month.

Playing is quick and easy with games every night! The questions are multiple choice and vary with randomized topics or subject-specific games. You will race against friends and players around the world to prove you’re the best, earn cash, and do good.

No money? No worries! You can enjoy our daily free games to get points, or earn money by referring friends. Join now for free, and get ready to start having fun while doing good with SHABBA!
