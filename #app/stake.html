Stake

Stake is a free tool that enables you to effortlessly keep track of shared expenses and chores with friends, roommates and family.

Create groups and categories to organize your tasks and expenses for better transparency.

Record points, time or money in just a few clicks.

Add a description or photo proofs if required, and leave comments on the recorded items.

Receive timely updates via Push Notifications and a real-time activity feed, so that you don't miss a thing.
