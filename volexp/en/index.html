F.A.Q.

Question 1. When is it appropriate to outsource foreign trade activities?
This solution is relevant in cases of one-time, non-systematic deliveries. In this case, you save on the maintenance of expensive competent professionals in the state.
Question 2. In what currencies is it advisable to conclude contracts with a Chinese supplier?
At this moment Russian banks offer the opening of foreign currency accounts in Chinese yuan. However, Chinese suppliers traditionally prefer long-term contracts in us dollars, as the yuan is unstable in the long term.
Question 3. What is the danger of wrong HS code selection to imported goods?
The correct imported goods classification at the contract stage approval is the key to the absence of problems with the Russian customs authorities, fraught with goods reclassification, administrative or criminal offenses and additional costs, which can subsequently exceed the planned profit many times.
Question 4. What is the best shipping route from China?
The optimal route depends on a number of factors and, above all, on the final destination in the Russian Federation, where the buyer would prefer delivery. According to this information, as well as the cargo volume and weight and the necessary delivery urgency the optimal route is chosen, often including several transport modes.

Customs clearance
and certification

Get services

Professional customs clearance of goods imported into the Russian Federation is an integral economic project part associated with the goods import from China.
Competently drawn up contract, commodity and transport documentation can reduce costs and minimize the risks of adjusting the customs value and additional customs duties charges, which can call into question the project profitability. Apart from that, for the goods transportation to the Russian territory it is necessary to provide the customs with certificates and other permissive documentation stipulated by the Russian Federation and the Customs Union law. We also undertake this task as our experts can organize the goods samples examination, determine the necessary permits type and formalize it in the appropriate authorized organizations.

Cargo carriage
organization

Get services

Despite some positive trends, shipping from China is still a complex and sometimes unpredictable process.
As is often the case, the Customer needs to make a choice between the speed and cost of delivery. Basing on your needs, our company offers the best delivery option.
In our work we highlight the following main directions of cargo transportation from China:

Shipping in prefabricated containers. This type is characterized by a budget delivery cost, but the time on the road is quite extended. It is also applicable for small shipments that do not require ultra-fast goods reception by the Client;

Combined road shipments. This service is slightly more expensive than the previous one, however, gives an opportunity to deliver goods in a shorter time and at any point specified by the Client;

Container shipments. This type of transportation is appropriate when sending a complete batch, multiple container load capacity / cargo capacity. Container transportation is carried out both by container trains following through the station Zabaikalsk and by mixed rail and water through the ports of the Far East with the subsequent sending by rail;

Road transportation. With the exception of air transport, this is the fastest and most predictable way to deliver large shipments from China. The volume of up to 120 m3, short travel time and safety of cargo contribute to the growing popularity of this transportation method from China over recent years.
Can not decide on the choice? Contact our experts for free advice on choosing the optimal cargo delivery scheme.

Complex foreign
economic activity outsourcing

Get services

In some cases, the maintenance of an own Department responsible for foreign economic activity is economically inexpedient. As a rule, this applies to one-time imports, samples import, the need for long-term goods shipment on an atypical route.

In this case, we can offer a full range of foreign trade outsourcing and act as a foreign trade agent for a transaction with a Chinese supplier.

Why is it profitable?
Shortest project implementation time;
No currency transactions and specialized reporting;
No interaction with customs authorities;
No costs for specialized employees: translators, logisticians, lawyers, declarants.
How does it work?

Firstly, you choose a supplier, or instruct us to select it on the basis of technical specifications and then we:

Prepare a foreign economic supply contract;
Offer a detailed delivery cost calculation on the optimal route;
Agree the terms of each batch readiness and the date of shipment with the supplier;
We check the accompanying documents for compliance with the requirements of customs and tax authorities, as well as currency control of the Russian Bank;
Organize transportation with strict declared terms observance;
Carry out goods samples examination and prepare cargo certificates necessary for import;
Arrange customs goods registration resolving all issues that may arise with the customs authorities;
Deliver the goods to the specified address after its release into free circulation;

Pass you the goods with a full set of documents that allow legally sell and use it on the territory of the Russian Federation and the countries of the customs Union.
